Medical Information

The purpose of medical science is to ensure the physical, mental, and social well-being of individuals. It aids in diagnosing, preventing, and treating diseases to help patients lead healthy lives. Medical science plays a vital role in improving the quality of life and advancing public health through preventive measures. Additionally, it contributes to the development of new treatments and innovative methods to provide safer and more effective healthcare for humanity.
